Vernier Library
Overview

VERNIER is a collection of C++ classes for estimating the pose of calibrated patterns with subpixel resolutions.

It includes the detection of periodic patterns, megarena patterns, QR-code-like and bitmap patterns.

VERNIER is written and maintained by researchers of the FEMTO-ST Institute located in Besançon, France.

VERNIER is hosted by Renater.

Authors: Antoine N. ANDRE, Guillaume J. LAURENT, Patrick SANDOZ
